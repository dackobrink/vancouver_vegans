Featured Restaurant

Meet on Main

100% Vegan Comfort Food

When the first location of this restaurant sprung up on Main Street it was the first of it's kind - a completely vegan restaurant that caters to your taste buds. That is delicious as it is ethical. Vancouver has been home to many grassroot health food plant based restaurants for many years . Meet on Main changed the stigma within the city, revealing that vegan food can be devilishly tastey. They've gained massive popularity since their onset, to the degree that they have a booming restaurant in the heart of Gastown. The walls of their restaurants are adorned in local art, creating a chic edge to the cozy decor.

Meet on Main continues to be the spot to decide from a cornocopeia of delicious burgers, nourishing pasta bowls or even nutritious salads.

The restaurant also boasts an expansive drink menu including their own hand-made vegan caesar.

Check them out in gastown or main street or Visit their website
