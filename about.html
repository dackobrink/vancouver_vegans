What we're about

The Vancouver Vegans website was created to fill the much needed space for the ever-expanding plant-based community within the lower mainland

    Our aim is to:
  • Support local businesses
  • Strengthen a community of like-minded individuals
  • Inspire those within in the lower mainland towards a vegan lifestyle

Vancouver hosts a large collection of reputable restaurants, stores and events that cater to vegan living. The site acts as a map and guide for these businesses throughout the city. The event calendar of the website reveals the activities of the various vegan groups, allowing participation in activism movement or information sessions. We use evidenced based information to inform through articles, interviews, social media and video. The city is host to many engaging activists, nutritionists, doctors and media creators who are taking stand for veganism and against animal cruelty. Many of these outstanding citizens contribute to the site by writing articles or using our calendar to broadcast their events.


We aim to be a part of the progressive environmental movement in the city by increasing awareness of ethical resources and businesses.
